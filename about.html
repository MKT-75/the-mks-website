Who I am

Hey, I’m MK, a student at Sutherland Secondary School, and I’m currently taking the Digital Communication course. I’ve always been fascinated by how digital platforms can shape communication, and I’m passionate about exploring new ways to tell stories, engage audiences, and create content. I believe in the power of digital media to drive change, spark creativity, and make ideas come alive.

What I’m Creating/Exploring

Right now, I’m focusing on designing this website and making it better. I’m especially interested in producing and editing video because I enjoy the process of producing an amazing video. In my coursework, I’m learning how to leverage digital tools to entertain as well as communicating with people online.

Why This Site Exists

This portfolio is a space where I showcase the work I’ve done as part of my Digital Communication course. It’s a way to demonstrate the skills I’ve developed and how I approach digital projects from start to finish. I hope it reflects my passion for communication in the digital world and serves as a resource for anyone interested in collaborating or learning more about digital media.
